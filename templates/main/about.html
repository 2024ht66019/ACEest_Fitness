About ACEest Fitness

Our Mission

ACEest Fitness & Gym Management System is designed to empower individuals on their fitness journey by providing comprehensive tracking, personalized insights, and actionable guidance.

We believe that fitness should be accessible, measurable, and motivating. Our platform combines modern technology with proven fitness principles to help you achieve your health and wellness goals.

What Makes Us Different

Science-Based Metrics

Automatic BMI, BMR, and TDEE calculations based on your profile, providing personalized health insights backed by scientific formulas.

Accurate Calorie Tracking

Uses MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values to calculate calories burned during each workout session accurately.

Visual Progress Tracking

Interactive charts and comprehensive analytics help you visualize your progress and stay motivated.

Key Features

  • Secure user authentication
  • Comprehensive profile management
  • Workout categorization (Warm-up, Workout, Cool-down)
  • Automatic calorie calculations
  • Exercise duration tracking
  • Progress visualization with charts
  • Personalized workout plans
  • Diet and nutrition guidance
  • Responsive mobile-friendly design
  • Historical workout data
